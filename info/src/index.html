WHAT IS RECYCLUSTER?

Recycluster is an enviroment centered app that revolves around the idea of recycling, giving its users the freedom to post recycle worthy material and to take it so it can be transformed into a new project, giving the materials a new life.

EMBLEM SYSTEM

recycluster is centered in a point-based system, allowing the users to quickly and effortlessly check the reliability of another user, earning points throughout the different interactions with the app, such as posting recycled projects.

FAQ

Is Recycluster free?

Yes, Recycluster is a free app, giving the user an easy to use experience, with an 'eco-friendly' mentality, Recycluster prioritazes the enviroment's wellbeing above everything else.

How do I earn points?

You can earn points by finishing a project and by interacting with different users on the platform, giving you the opportunity to improve your score by recycling, changing your score emblem as your score goes up.

Can I use Recycluster without posting anything?

Yes, you do not need to post in order to enjoy Recycluster's feed and interactive users, you can watch all new projects and interact with them even if you don't want to post anything.
